As you can see training fees are charged at 500 JPY per hour which means a 2 hour class would be 1,000 JPY and a 3 hour class would be 1,500 JPY.
Sports insurance is 1,850 JPY per annum for those who are high school students and older with a cap at 64 years of age.

上の通り一時間料金は500円なので2時間なのは1000円、3時間なのは1500円になります。
スポーツ保険の年間料金は1850円です。これで高校生から64歳までの方の道場内カバーになります。



Dogi
道着

Dogi are the uniform Shodokan practitioners are required to wear. Prices of which vary depending on quality, brand and whether or not Shodokan marks and logos come imprinted on them.
The table herebelow illustrates a price list of dogi that can be bought through the Shodokan Honbu dojo which include the Shodokan mark & logo.
