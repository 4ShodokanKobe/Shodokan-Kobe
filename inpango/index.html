INPANGO COMMERCIAL TRADING


We at Inpango Commercial Trading specialize in the importation of trucks, vans, trailers, fork lifts, lift trucks, cement mixers and other transport & construction equipment.

In addition to our vehicle and commercial equipment import department we specialize in the export of charcoal products and salt (for industrial use and for human consumption).

We are an Angolan based company trading with all Central, Western and Southern countries. We have been established in this region since 2001 and have achieved strong relationships with many companies in the above fields. (References available own request).
